Our Services

Specialized Services

Installation, assembly, testing, commissioning, and on-site support services for GIS and AIS systems in high- and extra-high-voltage substations throughout the Americas.

GIS and AIS Assembly and Installation

We provide comprehensive services in power transmission and distribution networks as well as in various types of industries. Specialists trained by sector manufacturers in medium, high, and extra-high voltage technologies across various models.

Technical Audits and Asset Assessments

Detailed diagnostics, risk analyses, performance comparisons, and strategic recommendations to improve reliability, safety, and operational efficiency of hydroelectric facilities and substations, in full compliance with IEC, IEEE, and ANSI standards.

Commissioning and Energization

Comprehensive commissioning, testing, and grid connection services for substations up to 500 kV, including AC and DC GIS systems for metro and railway applications.

Protection and Control Systems

Installation, configuration, testing, and parameterization of protection and control systems. We ensure optimal relay coordination, system selectivity, fault management, and grid reliability.

Testing and Verification

  • ▸ Functional and dielectric testing of instrument transformers (CTs and VTs)
  • ▸ Installation and commissioning of medium and high-voltage circuit breakers
  • ▸ Verification of disconnect switches and grounding switches
  • ▸ Preventive and corrective maintenance

Synchronization and Control Systems

Advanced platforms for automatic circuit breaker synchronization using proven methodologies to optimize system stability, maximize asset availability, and ensure safe infrastructure operation.

Our Strategic Direction

Our Vision

To establish SARTORI Energy as a global leader in the provision of specialized engineering services for high- and extra-high-voltage electrical infrastructure.

We aspire to be the trusted strategic partner for power generation, transmission, and distribution companies, EPC contractors, and industrial clients.

To contribute to the development of more efficient, safe, and sustainable substations, power grids, and energy systems through the application of specialized knowledge, continuous innovation, and a firm commitment to quality, safety, and operational performance.

Our Mission

To provide highly specialized engineering solutions for high- and extra-high-voltage GIS and AIS substations, executing each project with technical excellence, operational precision, and a firm commitment to quality, safety, and reliability.

We support our clients throughout the entire lifecycle of their assets, from installation and commissioning to maintenance, modernization, and long-term operational support.

By applying international standards, advanced technical expertise, and a culture of continuous improvement, we generate sustainable value for our clients and contribute to the development of power grids that are safer, more efficient, more reliable, and better prepared for the challenges of the global energy future.
